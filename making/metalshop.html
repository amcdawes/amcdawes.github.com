The mill is used to cut and shape work with rectangular symmetry. Additional equipment can be used to shape metal at specific angles. If you are used to using a drill press, this looks similar but can cut metal in addition to drilling holes.

The lathe is used to turn work with cylindrical symmetry. Aluminum, brass, steel, and plastic are the most common materials to use on this machine.

A wide range of hand tools are also available in the shop. Please inquire if you need something specific.
