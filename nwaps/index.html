Housing On campus

Rooms available in four- and six-room suites. Each suite includes shared bath facilities, kitchenette, and common areas. Suitemate requests are accepted, and we will do our best to honor such requests.

Travel Arrive in style

Forest Grove is on the west edge of the Portland metro area, and accessible from PDX airport via mass-transit (tri-met MAX Blue line). As the undergraduate campus for Pacific University, many college-town amenities are nearby.
